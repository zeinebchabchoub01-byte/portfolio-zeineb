Selected Projects

University of Utah

Doman Innovation Studio — Research Associate

At Doman Innovation Studio, our north star is helping founders tackle their “next-hardest step.” While coaching teams across campus, we encountered an abundance of programs, labs, mentors, and funding sources. Yet there was no easily accessible, high-level view that brought these resources together in one place. As a result, it was challenging for us to see the full picture of what existed across the University of Utah and to consistently guide founders toward the programs and support that best matched their needs at a given moment. The U Venture Ecosystem Map grew out of that insight.

What I did

  • Built an ecosystem strategy and mapping tool, covering 100+ programs across 30+ university entities.
  • Conducted 30+ conversations with individuals working across different programs, departments, and institutions on campus.

Results

Institutional perspective: The mapping process became an organizational learning tool. We surfaced how founder support actually operates day to day, not just how it is designed. Sharing early versions of the map aligned teams around a common goal: measuring success by founder outcomes, not program ownership.

Founder-facing perspective: The map shifted how we support founders—from explaining individual programs to actively guiding founders through the broader ecosystem, helping them access the right support, at the right time, from the right people. Orientation and navigation became as critical as programming.

Ecosystem perspective: By making the full system visible, the map created connective tissue across university programs. Instead of handoffs and referrals, teams could collaborate across units, align around founder goals, and operate as an integrated ecosystem.
